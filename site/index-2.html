Meet Doc Donn

Hello! This is Dr. Ben Donn. I'm hoping to become your personal dentist, so I'd like to share some information about myself. I grew up here in the Springs and graduated from Western State University, worked in IT for several years and then attended the University of Indiana School of Dentistry in Indianapolis. Subsequently, I completed a year of specialized training in dental implantology and I hold national certification in the American Academy of Implant Dentistry. In 2011 I returned to Colorado Springs, where I practiced complete care dentistry at North Powers Modern Dentistry. However, I decided being a personal "hometown doc" was a better fit for my personality. Consequently, I purchased the 40 year practice of Doctor Richard Mutchler in the Cheyenne Mountain /Broadmoor area, where I specialize in full-mouth restoration and full-time attention to your dental needs. My offices are immediately adjacent to Hwy 115 and convenient to Interstate 25.
