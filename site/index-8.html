Meet the Staff

Doc Donn loves to make you all laugh and hopefully not cry. He is your friendly neighboorhood dentist with one thing on his mind, helping you. His favorite pastimes are playing with swords, snowboarding in one piece ski suits, watching Walking Dead, and spending time with his family.

DENA, OFFICE MANAGER AND "Master" OF ALL TRADES

Dena is truly a "Master of all Trades".  She does whatever is needed to keep our office running smoothly and is "always there", from helping patients with insurance, assisting Dr. Donn, to helping elderly patients in or out of the chair.  The practice would be lost without this woman.  Her ideal date would include a night with Bon Jovi, the Broncos, her two kids and her husband... in that order


Meagan "Crow" Costantino, MASTER ASSISTANT

Meagan is Dr. Donn's right hand woman.  She is an expanded duties dental assistant who specializes in dental crowns, dentures, resin restorations, and implants.  Meagan loves dentistry and continues to study the art/science of dentistry on a daily basis.  In her free time she teaches Yoga.  Her favorite pose is "crow", which she had mastered.  She is an avid hiker and loves hiking in the snow with no sunscreen.  She recently hiked a vast section of Iceland, in the freezing rain! 

Nata, Master Hygienest

There is no safe place for plaque when Nata is on the job.  Nata is one of the most thorough hygienists, patients have ever met.  After mastering her trade for over 20 years at our practice, you will leave our office with your teeth feeling and looking as good as new.  Nata has worked in Germany in the past where she mastered the art of her craft.  There are no excuses for Nata and she is determined to whip her patients into great oral health shape!

Janet, Master Hygienist

Janet is passionate about oral health and health in general. She has mastered the sport of tennis.  She gave up her professional tennis career, to pursue her love of dentistry.  You will feel so fresh and clean after a hygiene appointment with Janet; guaranteed.
